🎬 Abbey Road Multi-User Video Annotator

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Data Collection Rules

  • COUNT ALL VEHICLES - Even when pedestrians are crossing!
  • Vehicles may be blocked, but they still arrived → COUNT THEM
  • Your job: Record ARRIVALS, not successful passages
  • The simulation will analyze delays using your timestamps

📹 Recording Data

  • Play the video while pressing buttons - Press AS events happen in real-time
  • Q/O = EB Vehicle arrives
  • W/P = WB Vehicle arrives
  • A/K = Pedestrian starts crossing
  • S = Crosser finishes (just crossing)
  • L = Poser finishes (stopped for photos)

💾 Exporting Data

  • Click 📄 CSV or 📊 Excel next to any table
  • Ignore any system security warnings - open the file anyway
  • Save immediately to your local drive
  • Files export in chronological order (oldest → newest)

✏️ Edit & Delete Functions

  • Edit Button: Click to modify timestamps or entity types
  • Delete Button: Click to remove incorrect entries
  • Undo Last (↩️): Quick removal of most recent entry

🎯 Key Point

Play video → Press buttons → Export → Save locally → Done!
